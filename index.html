Stylometric features

  • Average sentence length
  • Type-token ratio (TTR)
  • Burstiness index
  • Perplexity estimation
  • Shannon entropy
  • POS distribution
  • Readability scores
  • Lexical diversity

Limitations

No detector achieves perfect accuracy. Modern LLMs produce increasingly human-like output, causing irreducible error rates. This platform is designed for research evaluation—not adjudication. Results should be interpreted with epistemic humility.
